All About Bearded Dragons

What is a Bearded Dragon?

Bearded dragons are a type of lizard native to Australia. They are popular pets due to their friendly nature and unique appearance.

Fun Facts

  • Bearded dragons get their name from the "beard" of skin under their chin, which can puff out.
  • They are omnivores, eating both plants and insects.
  • Bearded dragons can change color to regulate their body temperature.
  • They enjoy basking in the sun and need UVB light in captivity.
  • Bearded dragons can live up to 10 years with proper care.

Care Tips

  • Provide a spacious tank with proper heating and lighting.
  • Feed a balanced diet of vegetables, fruits, and insects.
  • Keep their environment clean and offer fresh water daily.
